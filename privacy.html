2. Which data is processed

a) Uploaded images

When you upload a photograph, it is transmitted to external AI service providers (see Section 4) for the purpose of generating design suggestions and visualizations. The images are neither permanently stored on our servers nor used for training purposes. On the proxy server, they are held in memory only for the duration of the request.

Generated visualizations and product suggestions are stored in your device's local browser storage (IndexedDB) if you save them as a "Look". These data do not leave your device. They can be deleted at any time via your browser cache.

b) IP address

When the App is accessed and for every API request, your IP address is temporarily processed by the proxy server. This is done solely for the technical provision of the service and to prevent abuse (rate limiting: a maximum of 20 requests per minute, 200 per day per IP). The IP address is not persistently stored and not shared with third parties — the counters are held in memory only and automatically deleted after the limit window expires.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in stable, abuse-free operation).

c) Local storage in your browser

Faindz uses your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB for:

  • Language preference (DE/EN)
  • Marketplace selection (Amazon, Otto, Etsy)
  • Saved Looks and products (your library)

These data do not leave your device and are accessible only to you personally. This constitutes technically necessary storage under § 25(2) TTDSG (the German Telecommunications-Telemedia Data Protection Act); no consent is required.

4. External AI services (images & text analysis)

a) Anthropic (Claude AI)

The API of Anthropic, PBC (548 Market St PMB 90375, San Francisco, CA 94104, USA) is used to analyze your uploaded image and to generate product recommendations. According to Anthropic's own statements, image and text data is processed solely to respond to the request and is not used for model training. Privacy policy: anthropic.com/legal/privacy.

b) Google (Gemini)

The Gemini API of Google LLC (1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA) is used to generate image visualizations. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

c) Data transfers to the United States

The services mentioned above process data (also) in the United States. Anthropic, Google, and Vercel are either certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework or provide Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for the protection of personal data in accordance with Art. 46 GDPR. The legal basis for the transfer is Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR in conjunction with Art. 49(1)(b) (performance of contract).

5. Affiliate links

Faindz participates in the Amazon Associates program (and, where applicable, in further affiliate programs from Otto, Etsy, and others). When you click a product link, you are redirected to the respective merchant's website. On click, a unique partner ID and standard tracking parameters are transmitted to the destination shop.

Data processing on the destination site is subject to the respective merchant's privacy policy (e.g. Amazon: amazon.de privacy notice).

6. Cookies

Faindz does not set cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics. Only browser storage mechanisms (localStorage, IndexedDB) are used for the App's functionality, as described in Section 2.c.
